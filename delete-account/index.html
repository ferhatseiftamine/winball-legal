WinBall — Account & Data Deletion

Publisher: Scanlyne · Last updated: 17 July 2026

This page explains how to delete your WinBall account and the data associated with it.

Delete your data inside the app (fastest)

  1. Open WinBall.
  2. Go to Profile (the profile button on the main menu).
  3. Scroll to the Account section and tap “Delete account & data”.
  4. Confirm on the pop-up by tapping “Delete everything”.

This immediately and permanently erases the data WinBall stores about you.

What gets deleted

  • Your in-game nickname and avatar.
  • Your career statistics (goals and assists).
  • Your saved match replays.
  • Your settings and preferences.
  • The locally cached record of your VIP status.

What is managed by Google (not deleted by the app)

Some things are tied to your Google account rather than to WinBall, so you manage them through Google:

  • Google Play Games identity — controlled in your Google Play Games settings and Google account.
  • VIP subscription — cancel it in Google Play (Play Store → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions), or from inside WinBall via Profile → Manage subscription. Cancelling stops future billing.
