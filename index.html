How we work

Our own products

We build and publish our own games and apps, not client work. The roadmap is ours to get right, and we answer to the people who play them.

Small on purpose

A small team moves quickly and keeps taste consistent. Fewer people means fewer compromises between the idea and what actually ships.

The feel matters

Controls, motion, and the small moments get the most attention. That is usually the difference between something you try once and something you keep.
